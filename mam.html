Customer Policy

We provide reliable, professional cleaning services tailored to your needs, ensuring every space is spotless, fresh, and stress-free.

1. SUBSCRIPTION

Paused Subscription Policy

We understand that you may need to pause your cleaning subscription due to travel or other commitments. FlexRentz NeatClean offers flexible pause options, provided we are notified at least two (2) days in advance with the following details:

  • Start date of the pause period (Submit via your customer dashboard)
  • End date of the pause period (Submit via your customer dashboard)

Upon confirmation, we will pause your subscription for a period of one (1) to four (4) weeks per quarter. Your regular cleaning schedule will resume as soon as we receive notice of your availability.

PLEASE NOTE
  • No cleaning services will be provided once a subscription expires.
  • A paused subscription must not exceed four (4) weeks. If it does, the subscription is considered forfeited and a new payment is required to resume cleaning.
  • If the apartment is unattended or abandoned during the pause period, a deep cleaning service will be required before regular service can resume.

2. BOOKING

Cleaning Appointment Guidelines

We aim to ensure that our Cleaning Pros arrive on time for your scheduled service. At the time of booking, please indicate:

  • Whether you will be at home to receive the Pros
  • If a doorman or alternative access arrangement is available

Our Pros will wait up to 40 minutes at the location. If access is not granted within this time, the appointment will be forfeited and the Pros will be reassigned.

Important: Please ensure that all valuable items are put away prior to your cleaning appointment.

Rescheduling Policy

You will receive a reminder of your cleaning schedule 24 hours in advance via email or SMS. At this time, you may:

  • Confirm the scheduled appointment
  • Request to reschedule or cancel

No response indicates automatic confirmation, and the cleaning will proceed as scheduled.

PLEASE NOTE
  • Customers can cancel or reschedule without penalty by providing a written notice at least 24 hours before the scheduled service time.
  • Cancellations made at the time of visit will incur a 17% processing fee based on the total bill.
  • For FlexRentz Lite package holders: If our Pros are not allowed into the premises at the scheduled time, the session will be automatically forfeited.
