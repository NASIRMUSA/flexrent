Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. From pricing to scheduling, our FAQ covers it all.

+ Why hire FlexRentz Clean/Neat?

Because we go beyond cleaning — we transform your space and your peace of mind. At FlexRentz Clean/Neat, we combine expert cleaning, smart organization, and personalized care to deliver a service that fits perfectly into your lifestyle. Whether it's your home, office, or a special space, our trained team will tackle the mess and set up simple systems to keep things in order.

Plus, every client gets a dedicated relationship manager to ensure a smooth, stress-free experience from start to finish. We're fast, friendly, and focused on excellence — that's why we're the trusted choice across Africa.

+ Will my session be Confidential?

Yes, absolutely. Confidentiality is paramount. Our team adheres to strict privacy policies.

+ Can I request a refund?

Please refer to our cancellation and refund policy page for detailed information.

+ Do you Do pickup and Delivery?

Pickup and delivery services may be available for certain organizational tasks. Please inquire during your consultation.

+ What if in need to reschedule an Appointment

We understand things change. Please provide at least 24 hours notice for rescheduling to avoid any fees. Refer to our booking policy for details.

+ Which area of home do you clean?

We clean all standard residential areas including bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchens, living rooms, dining areas, and hallways. Specific requests can be discussed.

+ How do I get started with organizing?

Getting started is easy! Book a consultation, and we'll assess your space and discuss your goals to create a customized organization plan.

+ What takes place at the consultation?

During the consultation, we'll tour your space, discuss your needs and challenges, outline potential solutions and timelines, and provide a service estimate.

+ Are your services nationwide?

Currently, our services are focused on specific regions within Africa. Please check our service area map or contact us for availability in your location.

+ Couch and rug cleaning frequency?

Frequency depends on usage and household factors (pets, kids). Generally, professional cleaning is recommended every 12-18 months, with regular vacuuming in between.

+ What if something get damaged or stolen?

We are insured and bonded. In the unlikely event of damage or loss attributed to our service, please contact us immediately to initiate a claim process according to our terms and conditions.
